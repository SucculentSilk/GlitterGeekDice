Custom Dice

Custom projects are some of my favourite dice to make because it challenges my creativity and leads me to new discoveries.

Made with high quality, commercial grade resin, paired with eco friendly glitter; these dice are sure to please! Custom projects generally range from $80-$150 +shipping depending on the complexity. Often I make 1-2 test sets before you receive the final iteration. Custom projects generally take 1-2 weeks once the discussions surrounding what you envision are finalized. When you submit this form I’ll reach out to you in the next few business days to get a little bit more information from you.-- I’ll keep you updated along the way, and we can work together to create the dice that you envision!
